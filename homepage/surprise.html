Surprise

Did you know?

Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood.
Two of the hearts pump blood to the gills,
where it gets oxygenated, while the third heart
pumps the oxygenated blood to the rest of the body.
Their blood is blue due to the presence of hemocyanin,
which contains copper and is more efficient at
transporting oxygen in low-oxygen environments.
